FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about joining us.

What are your membership plans and pricing?

We offer Basic (PKR 5,000), Standard (PKR 7,500), and Premium VIP (PKR 12,000) packages. Our Premium VIP package includes a dedicated 1-on-1 personal trainer and customized diet plans.

Do you offer free trial sessions?

Yes! We offer a 1-day complimentary VIP trial pass for new residents of Karachi so you can experience our machines and atmosphere before committing.

Do beginners need prior gym experience?

Not at all. Over 50% of our new members are complete beginners. Our floor trainers will guide you through machine setups, proper posture, and initial workout routines.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday through Saturday from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM uninterrupted. Sundays are reserved for maintenance and deep sanitization.
